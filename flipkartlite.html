    Flipkart Plus

    A world of limitless possibilities awaits you - Flipkart Plus was kickstarted as a loyalty reward programme for all its regular customers at zero subscription fee. All you need is 500 supercoins to be a part of this service. For every 100 rupees spent on Flipkart order, Plus members earns 4 supercoins & non-plus members earn 2 supercoins. Free delivery, early access during sales and shopping festivals, exchange offers and priority customer service are the top benefits to a Flipkart Plus member.
    In short, earn more when you shop more!
